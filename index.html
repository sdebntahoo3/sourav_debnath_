About Me

I am Sourav Debnath, a graduate in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Khulna University, Bangladesh. My academic journey was rooted in signal processing and wireless communications, with hands-on research spanning 5G modulation systems, biomedical signal analysis, and IoT.


Currently, I serve as an Officer in the Information Technology Division at City Bank PLC — one of Bangladesh's most reputed financial institutions — where I was recognized with the Best Employee Award for the Data Center Migration Project. I remain actively engaged in collaborative research in AI, brain imaging, and reliable machine learning systems.
